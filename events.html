Chandigarh Startup Weekend

EIC PEC organises Chandigarh Startup Weekend, a two-day exclusive invite-only event that is a platform for all budding entrepreneurs to meet opportunities through investors, developers & marketing people. All of them come under a single roof to discuss ideas and share experiences. Ideas here can range from purely technical to design oriented to anything one can think of. It’s a place to experience if you love to dream. This is a perfect pedestal to showcase ones abilities in this field which makes this event one for which people strive hard enough to get the slots.

Ignite

Ignite is another one of many events organised by EIC annually. It is a competition which finally gives a chance to the enlightened minds to showcase what they have learned in thr workshops. One of the numerous ideas sent to the cell is selected and its innovator is awarded with a cash prize to convert the idea into a reality.

Pitching Competition

Followed by Ignite, a pitching competition is held after about a month where first entrepreneurs learn the art of pitching an idea under various experienced individuals and then finally practice this art to get a funding for their idea. The winner of this competition gets a chance to be associated with the Incubator and work on the idea under the right mentorship.

Apprentice

Apprentice is a one day event organised by EIC for the students of PEC University of Technology to help them learn about entrepreneurship from the experience of some great entrepreneurs from different fields. EIC has hosted personalities like Samar Singla, the CEO of Jugnoo as a guest speaker at this event. This is one of many events which gives a chance to the students to explore the different avenues and numerous opportunities the world holds for them.

Alpha Summit

EIC PEC exposes budding minds to an interaction session with established speakers from the business world. This is to provide help with a better understanding of how a start up works and the various aspects of building a business.

Innovex

In collaboration with CLIMBER, a UN recognized youth drive organization started by IIM Bangalore that focuses on helping students in being more confident and inspired by providing assistance ,a one day workshop will be held. The motive is to understand the tools that are required to build a startup and develop skills on how to use them for which the CEO, Mohammed Zeeshan, will be taking the workshop himself.
