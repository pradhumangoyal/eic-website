FRIEDEN ELECTRIC

Established in 2016 by PEC graduates, Frieden Electric develops innovative solutions with cutting edge technology for Indian markets. The latest solution offered is a Fully Automatic Kitchen Garden, which can grow upto 12 plants in a meagre 3 square feet. Entrepreneurship and Incubation Cell at the college has helped fine tune their ideas and give direction to their startup - say Satinder Sandhu and Jasmeet Randhawa, co founders of the company.

BRAIISET

Braiiset is a low cost braille printer. We do have braille printers available in the society we live in, but their cost is something that makes it inaccessible to a normal individual. With the invention of braiiset, the technology has been made available at a very low cost, and can change the lives of those unfortunate. Khushwant Singh, the founder of braiiset is currently interning in the United States.

QUAD-B

Quad-B is an idea that lets you work your innovation on a piece of cloth; it lets you design your own t-shirt at a very nominal price allowing you to have any sort of design you wish on a t-shirt.
